What are we?

SOVIC is the social responsibility cell of Assam Engineering College; working on inspiring youths to volunteer, instigating compassion into them and bestow them the tools to create transformative social change.

Next

Our Agenda

This initiative was started by a group of enthusiastic and socially committed students from Assam Engineering College Guwahati, hailing from diverse backgrounds with a common vision of social and community development. We aim to make a small but sustainable difference in society. AEC has always been a socially responsible institution and SOVIC is working as a flag bearer to carry forward its humanely legacy
